## 📝 Product Overview

**Bamboo Composite Decking** (Terrace Bamboo Flooring) is an elite, high-performance architectural solution engineered specifically for demanding outdoor environments. By fusing the natural strength of sustainable bamboo fibers with advanced manufacturing, natural materials are subjected to intense heat and immense hydraulic pressure, compressing them into an ultra-dense compound. Boasting an exceptional industrial density of **1250kg/m³**, this robust decking delivers superior structural strength, effectively preventing bending, cupping, or warping under harsh weather fluctuations.

This series features a distinguished **Profile E design**, meticulously crafted with a sleek, **smooth flat surface on both sides** for maximum versatility. The lateral edges are solid and without grooves, designed for secure top-down architectural installation. Available factory pre-oiled or non-oiled with premium eco-friendly weatherproofing treatments, this rich **Brown Toned Decking** offers flawless water resistance and advanced anti-mildew defenses, ensuring a long-life outdoor flooring option that stands up to severe weather.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our strand woven outdoor bamboo composite decking is manufactured utilizing sustainable raw Moso bamboo harvested at peak maturity from China's premier ecological forestry hubs across **Anhui**, Jiangxi, Fujian, and Zhejiang, centered around our pristine source base in the **Huangshan Mountain** range.

1. **Fiber Extraction & Thermal Conditioning:** Raw Moso bamboo is split into premium structural strips and mechanically processed into fine, pliable bamboo fiber bundles.
2. **Advanced Thermal Carbonization:** The extracted fibers undergo deep high-temperature steam carbonization, caramelizing internal starches to achieve the natural deep brown hue while purging nutrients to resist fungal growth.
3. **High-Stress Stranded Compression:** Immersed in non-toxic, eco-conscious marine-grade binding formulations, the fibers are bundled and subjected to immense hydraulic cross-weaving and intense compression to achieve the targeted 1250kg/m³ density.
4. **Automated CNC Milling:** The solidified timbers pass through high-precision automated milling lines to shape the iconic smooth flat surface profile on both sides and clean solid edges.
5. **Protective Surface Treatment:** Finished with either high-grade exterior oils for immediate weatherproofing or left non-oiled per customer specifications for versatile deployment.
