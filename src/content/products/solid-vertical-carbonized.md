## 📝 Product Overview

**Solid Bamboo Flooring** is a premium, eco-elegant surfacing solution crafted from sustainable Moso bamboo. Characterized by its vertical construction, the bamboo strips are oriented on-edge and side-pressed. This technique significantly minimizes the visibility of natural bamboo "knuckles," yielding a cleaner, seamlessly flowing linear grain pattern that brings a touch of sleek, contemporary flair to any space.

This series features a deep **Carbonized (Caramel) color finish**. Through a specialized high-temperature steaming and carbonization process, the sugars within the fibers are caramelized, giving the bamboo a rich, amber hue that radiates warmth and sophistication. Sealed with a non-toxic, eco-friendly UV lacquer, it stands out as an exceptional, ultra-low formaldehyde alternative to traditional solid hardwoods.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our solid bamboo flooring is engineered using raw Moso bamboo sourced directly from the heart of China's premium ecological plantations, spanning across **Anhui**, Jiangxi, Fujian, and Zhejiang provinces, with the pristine **Huangshan Mountain** region as our core plantation hub.

1. **Precision Splitting & Sorting:** Mature Moso bamboo is split into premium strips and planed to precise tolerances.
2. **Specialized Thermal Carbonization:** The bamboo strips undergo prolonged exposure to high-pressure steam. This carbonization process deepens the material into a rich caramel color while optimizing its defense against wood-boring insects.
3. **Eco-Friendly Adhesion:** Coated evenly with premium, non-toxic, eco-conscious adhesives.
4. **Side-Pressed Heavy Laminating:** Bundled tightly on-edge and subjected to immense hydraulic pressure (Vertical orientation) to lock in superior structural strength.
5. **CNC Profiling & Protective Coating:** Milled with specialized automated lines for a flawless Tongue & Groove interlocking fit, and factory-finished with durable UV lacquer coatings.
