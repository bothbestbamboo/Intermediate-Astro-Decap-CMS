## 📝 Product Overview

**Click Lock Strand Woven Bamboo Flooring** represents a premier achievement in modern sustainable interior architecture, engineered to offer a luxurious yet remarkably durable alternative to traditional exotic hardwoods. By combining the exceptional structural performance of high-density strand woven bamboo with a glueless installation profile, this flooring incorporates the precision-engineered **Unilin click lock system**. This allows the planks to lock together effortlessly, eliminating the need for nails, staples, or messy chemical adhesives during a floating installation.

The distinct **Carbonized Color** (also known as rich caramel or warm honey tone) is achieved through an advanced thermal steaming process that darkens the natural sugars deep within the bamboo fibers without relying on artificial dyes or surface stains. Boasting an ultra-dense solid profile of **1050 kg/m³**, this high-performance flooring delivers magnificent dimensional stability and excellent resistance against daily wear and indentation, bringing modern eco-elegance and inviting warmth to any high-end layout.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our carbonized click lock strand woven bamboo flooring is carefully engineered using premium Moso bamboo stalks grown in sustainably managed ecological zones throughout **Anhui Province**, with our main production infrastructure centered around the pristine **Huangshan Mountain** region.

1. **Fiber Stripping & Purification:** Mature bamboo stalks are cut into sections and mechanically opened into long, cross-linked fiber strands.
2. **Thermal Carbonization:** The strands undergo high-pressure steaming to caramelize the inherent starches, transforming the material into a beautiful honey-brown tone while extracting sugars that typically attract pests.
3. **Eco-Friendly Infusion:** The carbonized fibers are deeply coated with high-grade, eco-conscious binding formulas that meet strict low-VOC indoor air safety standards.
4. **Hydraulic High-Heat Pressing:** The treated fiber strands are meticulously arranged and placed into high-stress hydraulic press lines to fuse the fibers into dense, solid bamboo logs.
5. **Click Profiling & UV Coating:** The logs are sliced into planks and milled via precision automated CNC machinery into the exact Unilin click locking profile, followed by multi-layered UV-cured lacquer applications.
