## 📝 Product Overview

**Single-Layer Horizontal Carbonized 5mm Bamboo Panel** (also known as single-layer flat-pressed bamboo sheets or architectural veneers) is a high-density, eco-luxury surfacing material engineered for sleek interior cladding, furniture backings, premium cabinetry facing, and laser/CNC design work. By flat-pressing selected Moso bamboo strips in a unified horizontal orientation, this thin-profile panel beautifully showcases the traditional, prominent structural bamboo "knuckles" and natural organic nodes repeating across its face.

This specialized series features a sleek **5mm thickness engineered as a single-layer horizontal solid board**, perfectly combining high flexibility with the immense tensile strength of mature bamboo. To achieve its elegant **Carbonized (Caramel / Amber Toned) finish**, the raw bamboo strips are high-pressure steamed for 15 minutes, caramelizing the plant's natural sugars to lock in a deep, uniform hue throughout the entire thickness. Pressed under massive hydraulic force using zero-emission bonding agents, these unfinished 2440x1220mm sheets provide a sustainable, modern alternative to traditional hardwood veneers, outperforming regular woods in surface hardness.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our heavy-duty thin industrial bamboo panels are engineered using premium Moso bamboo stalks harvested at peak maturity from ecological forestry zones in **Anhui**, Jiangxi, Fujian, and Zhejiang provinces, with our main extraction base nestled within the **Huangshan Mountain** range.

1. **Precision Slicing & Planing:** Raw mature bamboo poles are cut, split, and planed into fine, uniform rectangular strips.
2. **Pot-Steaming Carbonization:** The bamboo strips are placed in a high-pressure treatment pot and steamed for 15 minutes to fully caramelize internal starches, safeguarding the panel from future wood-boring pests while locking in the rich amber color.
3. **Kiln Drying & Sanding:** Strips are kiln-dried to an optimized, uniform moisture content and sanded flat.
4. **Cross-Laminated Single-Layer Matrix:** Coated evenly with premium non-toxic adhesive matrices, the strips are arranged in parallel, flat-pressed layers to form a unified, single-ply solid sheet.
5. **Heavy Hydraulic Hot-Pressing:** The aligned layers are subjected to immense hydraulic pressure and heat-cured into solid, stable 2440x1220x5mm sheets.
