## 📝 Product Overview

**Vertical Carbonized 5mm Bamboo Panel** (also known as single-layer side-pressed bamboo sheets or veneers) is a high-density, eco-luxury surfacing material engineered for sleek interior cladding, furniture backings, custom cabinetry facing, and laser/CNC design work. By orienting premium Moso bamboo strips on edge in a unified vertical alignment, this thin-profile slab minimizes the visibility of traditional bamboo "knuckles," providing a sophisticated, continuous, and flowing fine-line linear grain appearance across its surface.

This specialized series features a sleek **5mm thickness engineered as a single-layer vertical board**, blending flexibility with the inherent tensile strength of mature bamboo. To achieve its elegant **Carbonized (Caramel / Amber Toned) finish**, the raw bamboo strips are high-pressure steamed for 15 minutes, perfectly caramelizing the plant's natural sugars to lock in a deep, uniform hue throughout the entire thickness. Pressed under massive hydraulic force using zero-emission bonding agents, these unfinished 2440x1220mm sheets provide a sustainable, modern alternative to traditional hardwood veneers, outperforming regular woods in surface hardness.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our heavy-duty thin industrial bamboo panels are engineered using premium Moso bamboo stalks harvested at peak maturity from ecological forestry zones in **Anhui**, Jiangxi, Fujian, and Zhejiang provinces, with our main extraction base nestled within the **Huangshan Mountain** range.

1. **Precision Slicing & Planing:** Raw mature bamboo poles are cut, split, and planed into fine, uniform rectangular strips.
2. **Pot-Steaming Carbonization:** The bamboo strips are placed in a high-pressure treatment pot and steamed for 15 minutes to fully caramelize internal starches, safeguarding the panel from future wood-boring pests while locking in the rich amber color.
3. **Kiln Drying & Sanding:** Strips are kiln-dried to an optimized, uniform moisture content and sanded flat.
4. **Side-Pressed Matrix Alignment:** Coated evenly with premium non-toxic adhesive matrices, the strips are oriented on edge (vertical grain) in a parallel single-layer alignment to form a unified sheet.
5. **Heavy Hydraulic Hot-Pressing:** The aligned layers are subjected to immense hydraulic pressure and heat-cured into solid, stable 2440x1220x5mm sheets.
