## 📝 Product Overview

**Fossilized Strand Woven Bamboo Flooring** represents the pinnacle of modern eco-conscious floor engineering, offering a luxurious wide-plank aesthetic that matches or surpasses the toughest exotic hardwoods. Utilizing an advanced fiber reorganization process, raw Moso bamboo stalks are meticulously split, refined into long cross-linked fiber strands, and fused together under immense hydraulic pressure and extreme heat with premium low-VOC ecological binding matrices.

This particular profile features a generous **130mm width**, providing a seamless, high-end look that expands the visual volume of any interior space. The **Blonde Natural Color** yields exceptionally clean, light-toned hues that preserve the inherited botanical elegance and earthy charm of raw bamboo, delivering an airy, minimalist, and thoroughly modern eco-elegance. With its hyper-dense **1050 kg/m³** solid structure, this flooring offers magnificent dimensional stability and resistance against everyday indentation.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our wide-plank fossilized bamboo flooring is crafted from sustainably managed Moso bamboo species native to premium ecological regions across **Anhui Province**, with our primary manufacturing lines nestled around the scenic **Huangshan Mountain** range.

1. **Fiber Harvesting & Separation:** Mature bamboo sections are precision-cut and mechanically drawn into long, interconnected fiber strands.
2. **Thermal Sugars Extraction:** The structural strands are boiled in a pristine aqueous conditioning solution to strip away natural starches and sugars, rendering the core material completely immune to termites and micro-biological decay.
3. **Co-Polymer Binding Coating:** The purified blonde strands are infused with high-grade, eco-conscious binding compounds.
4. **Extreme Fossilized Compression:** The strands are tightly woven and placed into heavy hydraulic press blocks where intense heat and force compress the fibers into incredibly dense, solid bamboo timbers.
5. **Precision CNC Profiling:** Automated profiling machinery accurately mills the flawless Tongue & Groove interlocking joints, followed by a multi-layered UV lacquer application line.
