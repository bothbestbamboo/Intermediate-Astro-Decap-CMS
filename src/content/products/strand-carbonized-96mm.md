## 📝 Product Overview

**Strand Woven Bamboo Flooring** stands as a premier achievement in sustainable interior architecture, engineered to offer a luxurious yet highly durable alternative to rare and slow-growing exotic hardwoods. Sourced from rapidly renewable Moso bamboo, this flooring undergoes a state-of-the-art fiber reorganization process. Raw bamboo beams are meticulously split and conditioned into long, cross-linked fiber strands, which are then fused together under immense hydraulic pressure and high temperatures using premium, eco-conscious low-VOC adhesive systems.

The distinct **Carbonized Color** (often called caramel or rich honey tone) is achieved through an advanced thermal carbonization process, which darkens the natural sugars deep within the bamboo fibers without any artificial dyes or chemical stains. Boasting an ultra-dense profile of **1050 kg/m³**, this flooring exhibits unparalleled dimensional integrity and compression resistance, bringing a deeply inviting, warm, and sophisticated modern aesthetic to any interior layout.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our carbonized strand woven bamboo flooring is carefully processed using premium Moso bamboo grown in sustainably managed ecological zones across **Anhui Province**, with our main production and plantation supply chains anchored in the pristine **Huangshan Mountain** region.

1. **Fiber Stripping & Purification:** Mature bamboo stalks are carefully split into long strips and opened into cross-linked fiber strands.
2. **Thermal Carbonization:** The strands are placed in high-pressure steaming chambers to caramelize the inherent starches, transforming the material into a beautiful honey-brown tone while extracting sugars that typically attract termites.
3. **Eco-Conscious Infusion:** The carbonized fibers are deeply infused with safe, low-VOC binding formulas.
4. **Hydraulic High-Heat Pressing:** The treated fiber strands are meticulously woven and subjected to immense hydraulic press lines to form dense, uniform bamboo logs.
5. **Milling & UV Coating:** The logs are sliced and precisely milled via CNC machinery into clean Tongue & Groove planks before receiving durable UV-cured lacquer coatings.
