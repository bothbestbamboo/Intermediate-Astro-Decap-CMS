## 📝 Product Overview

**Solid Bamboo Flooring** is premium-crafted from rapidly renewable Moso bamboo. By side-pressing a single layer of bamboo strips oriented on edge, this vertical construction significantly reduces the visibility of traditional botanical "knuckles," providing a much cleaner, uniform, and flowing linear appearance across the room.

Finished with a high-grade, non-toxic pre-finished UV lacquer, it delivers a very light-toned, bright, and organic aesthetic that perfectly preserves the inherited natural and earthy look of botanical bamboo. This makes it an exceptional, ultra-low formaldehyde alternative to traditional solid wood and hardwoods.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our solid bamboo flooring is engineered using raw Moso bamboo sourced directly from the heart of China's premium ecological plantations, spanning across **Anhui**, Jiangxi, Fujian, and Zhejiang provinces, with the pristine **Huangshan Mountain** region as our core plantation hub.

1. **Precision Splitting & Planing:** Mature bamboo stalks are selected and precisely split into uniform rectangular strips.
2. **Thermal Steaming & Conditioning:** The strips undergo high-temperature treatment to regulate natural sugars and moisture, ensuring resistance to pests and warping.
3. **Eco-Friendly Adhesion:** Combined with non-toxic, eco-friendly glue matrices.
4. **Precision Side Pressing:** Formed using rigorous high-pressure line pressing where bamboo strips are turned on edge vertically and pressed together into a solid structure.
5. **CNC Profiling & Finish Coating:** Milled via precision automated profiling for flawless Tongue & Groove interlocking joints, and coated with multi-layered protective UV lacquers.
