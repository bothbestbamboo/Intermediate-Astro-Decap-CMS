## 📝 Product Overview

**Click Lock Strand Woven Bamboo Flooring** represents a significant technological advancement in modern sustainable flooring. Combining the incredible physical performance of high-density strand woven bamboo with the effortless efficiency of a glueless profile, this flooring incorporates the world-renowned **Unilin click lock system**. This allows the planks to interlock seamlessly for a floating installation, drastically reducing labor time and eliminating the need for messy adhesives or nails.

Sourced from premium Moso bamboo, the raw stalks are processed using advanced reorganization methods. The bamboo fibers are separated into cross-linked bundles and compressed under intense hydraulic pressure and thermal processing to achieve an exceptional density of **1050 kg/m³**. The **Natural Color** options deliver very light-toned, bright golden aesthetics that preserve the inherited botanical elegance and earthy look of raw bamboo, providing a clean, spacious feel to any upscale interior.

## 💎 Key Advantages

* **Effortless Unilin Click System:** Features precision-engineered click lock profiles that allow planks to snap together firmly without glue, making it an excellent choice for efficient residential and commercial floating installations.
* **High-Performance Wear Resistance:** With a Janka Hardness rating exceeding 2,000 pounds-force, its compressed structure is significantly harder than premium traditional hardwoods like oak, maple, ash, and hickory, making it highly resilient against impacts.
* **Pure Botanical Aesthetics:** The natural blonde tones provide bright, airy, and organic aesthetics, serving as a beautiful canvas that effortlessly complements modern, Scandinavian, or minimalist decor.
* **Excellent Climate Stability:** The multi-directional pressing of high-density cross-linked fibers paired with the floating click design drastically minimizes the effects of seasonal humidity shifts, preventing gaps or warping.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our click lock strand woven bamboo flooring is carefully engineered using premium Moso bamboo stalks grown in sustainably managed ecological zones throughout **Anhui Province**, with our main production infrastructure centered around the pristine **Huangshan Mountain** region.

1. **Fiber Stripping & Sugar Extraction:** Mature bamboo stalks are cut into strips and mechanically separated into long, cross-linked fiber strands. These are boiled in a pristine aqueous solution to extract natural sugars, ensuring the bamboo is completely immune to termites and biological decay.
2. **Thermal Conditioning & Drying:** The purified fibers undergo rigorous moisture management and oven drying to lock in future dimensional integrity.
3. **Eco-Friendly Infusion:** The natural strands are deeply coated with high-grade, eco-conscious binding formulas that meet strict low-VOC indoor air safety standards.
4. **Hydraulic High-Heat Pressing:** The treated fiber strands are meticulously arranged and placed into high-stress hydraulic press lines to fuse the fibers into dense, solid bamboo logs.
5. **Click Profiling & UV Coating:** The logs are sliced into planks and milled via precision automated CNC machinery into the exact Unilin click locking profile, followed by multi-layered UV-cured lacquer applications.
