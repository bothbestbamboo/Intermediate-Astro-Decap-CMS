## 📝 Product Overview

**Strand Woven Bamboo Flooring** represents an innovative architectural milestone in sustainable building materials, delivering a high-performance alternative to traditional exotic hardwoods. Crafted from sustainably harvested Moso bamboo, the raw material undergoes an advanced reorganization process. The bamboo stalks are meticulously split and drawn into elongated, cross-linked fiber bundles, which are then compressed under intense hydraulic pressure and high temperatures with premium low-VOC matrices.

Engineered to an exceptional density of **1050 kg/m³**, this high-density engineering grade flooring offers incredible dimensional stability and structural strength, making it resilient against warping, cupping, or bending. The **Natural Color** profile features very light-toned, bright golden grain patterns that beautifully showcase the inherited botanical elegance and earthy charm of Moso bamboo, filling indoor spaces with clean, sustainable, and modern warmth.

## 🏭 Manufacturing Process

Our strand woven bamboo flooring is engineered using premium Moso bamboo sourced from ecologically balanced, sustainably managed plantations primarily across **Anhui Province**, with our core logistics and manufacturing networks centered around the pristine **Huangshan Mountain** range.

1. **Fiber Separation & Sugars Extraction:** Mature Moso bamboo stalks are split into strips and mechanically separated into long, pliable fiber bundles. These are boiled in a specialized solution to extract natural starches and sugars, ensuring complete biological immunity against termites and fungal growth.
2. **Thermal Conditioning & Drying:** The purified fibers undergo exact moisture monitoring and oven drying to lock in future dimensional performance.
3. **Cross-Linked Composing:** Immersed in eco-conscious, low-VOC binding matrices, the bamboo fibers are aligned and woven into dense structural blocks.
4. **Intense Hydraulic Compression:** The composite blocks are placed under intense high-stress hydraulic pressing lines and extreme temperatures to form solid, dense bamboo timbers.
5. **CNC Joint Milling & Coating:** The solid timbers pass through precision automated CNC lines to cut the exact Tongue & Groove profile, followed by multi-layered UV lacquer applications.
